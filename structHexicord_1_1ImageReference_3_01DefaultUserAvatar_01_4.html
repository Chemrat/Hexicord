constexpr bool Hexicord::ImageReference< DefaultUserAvatar >::isAnimated ( ) const

Check whatever image is animated (GIF).

Animated avatars can be download()'ed as PNG or other regular format, but attempt to download regular avatar as GIF will throw LogicError.

Always false for ImageType other than UserAvatar.
